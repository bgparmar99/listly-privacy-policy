Delete Your Listly Account & Data

Last updated: June 23, 2026

You can request deletion of your Listly account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to submit a request, what data is removed, and what may be retained.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to us from the email address associated with your Listly account:

Deletion request

Email: Orbitralabs@gmail.com

Subject line: Delete My Account

Send from: The email address you used to sign up for Listly

We will verify your identity and process the request. You do not need to provide any additional documentation.

What Gets Deleted

Upon verification, we permanently delete:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile (display name and email address)
  • Lists you created and all their contents
  • Your memberships in shared lists
  • Items, prices, and notes you added
  • Expense splits and settlements you were part of
  • Analytics identifiers associated with your account

What May Be Retained

Some information may be retained where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes, or in anonymised form that can no longer identify you. Shared data created collaboratively — for example, a shared list still in active use by other members — may be retained for those other users, with your personal attribution removed.

Processing Time

🕐 We process deletion requests within 30 days.

You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.
